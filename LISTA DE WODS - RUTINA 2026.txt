WOD-01: DT adaptado
Calentamiento (5min):
AMRAP 5min:

10 Air Squats
10 Jumping Jacks
10 Leg Raises
5 Burpees (sin push-up)

WOD Principal (15min):
5 rounds for time:

12 DB Deadlifts
9 DB Hang Power Cleans
6 DB Push Jerks
DBs moderados (15-20kg c/u)
